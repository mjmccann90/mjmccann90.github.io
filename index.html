About Me

I am a Nashville native who graduated from Lipscomb University in 2015 with a degree in Public Communications and Leadership. Upon graduating, I joined Pella Windows and Doors as a Sales and Customer Service representative. While I excelled and enjoyed many aspects of the role, I realized I wanted more to take my career in a different direction. I was introduced to programming by a friend and found the logical and problem solving aspects intriguing. After completing a three-week Jumpstart course at Nashville Software School, I knew this was the path I needed to be on. Now enrolled in the full-time Bootcamp, I can genuinely say that this career change has been one of the best decisions in my life. I am excited to be a part of the Nashville tech community.
